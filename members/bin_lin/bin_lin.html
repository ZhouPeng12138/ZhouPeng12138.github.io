About Me


Bin Lin got her doctoral degree in Agricultural Economics and Management from the Zhejiang University (P.R. China).

She is currently a postdoctoral research fellow and also a member of the MAgPIE-China Research Group. Her research interests include Food Systems, Nitrogen Management, Productivity & Efficiency Analysis, Economic Modeling, and Agricultural & Environmental Economics.

She works on both econometric models and MAgPIE-China (a country-specific version of the Model of Agricultural Production and its Impact on the Environment for China) to analyze various research topics. She has published papers in World Development, Sustainability Science, Environmental Research Letters, and Environmental Impact Assessment Review.
